International Cuisine

Sri Lanka is known for its rich and diverse culinary traditions, but it also offers a range of international cuisines due to its growing tourism industry and multicultural population. There are many popular international cuisines you can find in Sri Lanka such as Indian Cuisine, Chinese Cuisine, Italian Cuisine, Japanese Cuisine, Thai Cuisine, Middle Eastern and Western Cuisine.

Indian Food

Traditional Indian food is renowned worldwide for its wonderful use of herbs and spices, and its diverse range of deep-fried snacks, pastries, curries, gravies, sauces, rice dishes, tandoor-cooked meats, vegetable dishes, chutneys, breads and sweets.



Italian Food

Italian food is near the heart for many of us, and normal Italian dishes are ordinary in practically all cafés, from lasagnas to pizzas and pasta. The assortment offered in such cafés is very restricted to the more well-known choices, so discovering an eatery or restaurant that serves just Italian is now and then elusive.
